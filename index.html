What's In Store

High Speed Thrills

Jet Skis

Grip the handlebars, hit the throttle, and tear across the crystal-clear waters of Nassau Harbour. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned rider, our jet skis deliver an adrenaline rush unlike anything else — with the stunning Bahamian coastline as your backdrop.

Sky High Views

Parasailing

Soar up to 400 feet above the turquoise sea and take in breathtaking panoramic views of Nassau and Paradise Island. It's peaceful, exhilarating, and absolutely unforgettable — the ultimate way to see the Bahamas from a whole new perspective.

Underwater World

Snorkeling

Dip beneath the surface and discover a vibrant world of coral reefs, tropical fish, and stunning sea life. Our guided snorkeling excursion takes you to some of Nassau's most beautiful reef spots — gear included, no experience needed.

Group Fun

Banana Boat

Hold on tight and laugh until your sides hurt! Our banana boat ride is the perfect group activity — friends, family, kids, everyone gets soaked and everyone has a blast. The only question is: how long can you hang on before you hit the water?

Only in the Bahamas

Swimming Pigs

Yes, you read that right. Meet the world-famous swimming pigs of the Bahamas and swim alongside them in their natural habitat. It's quirky, it's joyful, and it makes for the kind of story and photos you'll be sharing for years to come.
